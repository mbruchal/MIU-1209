Beginner Day 1

System is total but will vary. Warm up with 5 minutes of stretching.

  • Prone Triceps (Skull Crushers): 3 x 12 superset with:
  • Barbell curls: 3 x 12+ if possible
  • Flat bench presses: 3 x 12/15 superset with:
  • Single arm rows: 3 x 10/12
  • Squats: 3 x 15/20
  • Press behind the neck: 3 x 12/15 superset with:
  • Barbell shrugs: 3 x 8/10

    • Abs

  • Seated barbell twists: 2 minutes
  • Crunches for 2 minutes or failure

    Mon + Tue

    These days are all about your shoulders, back, and triceps. Go as heavy as possible in both sets. Warm up for at least 5 minutes.

  • Seated behind the neck shoulder press: 2 x 8
  • Dumbell lateral raises: 2 x 10
  • Single arm rows: 2 x 10
  • Deadlifts: 2 x 10
  • Dips: 2 x to failure
  • Tricep kickbacks: 2 x 10 one arm at a time

    • Abs

  • Crunches: 2 x to failure
  • Decline sit-ups: 2 x to failure
